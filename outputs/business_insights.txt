BUSINESS INSIGHTS

Cluster 0:
Regular customers with medium spending habits.
Recommended Strategy:
- Cashback offers
- Discount coupons

Cluster 1:
Premium customers with high balances and frequent transactions.
Recommended Strategy:
- Premium credit cards
- Higher credit limits
- Exclusive rewards

Cluster 2:
Customers with very high credit usage and lower repayment rates.
Recommended Strategy:
- EMI plans
- Credit risk monitoring
- Financial awareness programs

Cluster 3:
Responsible customers with lower balances and better repayment behavior.
Recommended Strategy:
- Loyalty programs
- Personalized offers
- Small credit limit increase
